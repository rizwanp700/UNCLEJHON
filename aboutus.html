About

UNCLE JOHN, THE UNIQUE ICE CREAM BRAND

Synonym for splendid sweetness, Uncle John reigns the world of ice creams today. This most celebrated ice cream brand was named thus as a tribute to Mr. M C John, who made his own kingdom of ice creams in Kerala five decades back in 1972. Serving Kerala State Government as a Senior Bureaucrat for a long period, Mr. M C John took the great step of pursuing his dream to become an entrepreneur after voluntary retirement and transformed into an ‘Icon of Ice Cream Industry’ in due course. After establishing South India's pioneering ice cream brand - Uncle John, new brand additions like Skei were introduced in the coming years by Kreemfoods (P) Ltd founded by himself. Uncle John immediately became a fast-selling ice cream brand with outstanding taste and impeccable quality. Headquartered in Kochi, Uncle John continues its supremacy in the ice cream industry with 13 factories spanning across India over the past 50 years. Uncle John also has an overseas brand impact through its operations and production cantered in Africa.

LOCALIZED MANUFACTURING PROCESS

Strategically located at every major city in South India, Uncle John ice cream factories work on the basis of stand-alone units, specialized in reflecting local tastes and producing ample quantity of ice cream for the particular region. This kind of infrastructure enables the brand to meet localized needs in no time and to deliver factory fresh ice creams within a short period of ordering. All the activities of the local plants are guided and controlled by the centralized operating system while innovation and latest technology rule the process of production and marketing.

THE WIDEST FACTORY NETWORK

Uncle John Ice Creams operates nine factories in Kerala located at Kannur, Kozhikode, Palakkad, Thrissur, Ernakulam, Kottayam, Kollam, Pathanamthitta & Trivandrum, six factories in Tamil Nadu located at Chennai, Madurai, Tiruchy, Pondicherry, Tirunelveli & Erode, two factories in Karnataka located at Mangalore & Bangalore and one factory in Telengana located at Hyderabad. Overseas production is based in Congo, Central Africa at Kinshasa.
